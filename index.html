Welcome! Are you ready to hear a DNA duet? We made it possible by translating triplets of bases into musical information. Enter base strings on each side and select the instruments. Hit play all, sit back and enjoy.



 

About

Sonifying DNA using MIDI.js for the USC "Let the Data Speak".

Optimised for Firefox and a resolution of 1920x1080 Pixels.
